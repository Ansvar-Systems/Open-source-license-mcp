## Not Legal Advice

This software provides informational data about open-source software licenses, compatibility analysis, and regulatory obligations. **It does not constitute legal advice** and no attorney-client relationship is created by its use.

## Verification Required

All compliance conclusions produced by this tool must be verified by qualified legal counsel before acting on them. Specific limitations:

- **Compatibility matrix** covers 26 common license pairings. Uncovered combinations return `no_data` — this does not mean the licenses are incompatible; it means no curated determination is available.
- **Obligation checklists** cover 15 high-use licenses. Other licenses may have obligations not represented in this database.
- **CRA (Cyber Resilience Act) obligations** are informational summaries, not authoritative EU legal guidance. Consult the [Official Journal of the EU](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/) and qualified legal advisors for binding interpretations.
- **REUSE specification rules** are derived from the FSFE REUSE Specification 3.3. Verify current rules at [reuse.software](https://reuse.software/).
- **NOTICE templates** are guidance — your specific project structure and dependency graph may require different attribution formats.
